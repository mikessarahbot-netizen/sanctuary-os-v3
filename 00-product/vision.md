# Vision

**Sanctuary OS** replaces the fragmented church-tools stack (planning · charts · tracks · slides · communications · livestream) with one connected, volunteer-friendly system.

## Target user
Worship leaders, pastors, musicians, production volunteers, and admins at small-to-mid churches with limited tech staff.

## Principles
- Simplicity > cleverness
- Reliability > feature count
- One workflow > five loosely connected ones
- AI assists, not decides
- Privacy is a product requirement
- Every automation path must fail gracefully

## Non-goals
General-purpose CRM · accounting/giving platform · sermon manuscript editor · full DAW · generic livestream hosting (v1)
